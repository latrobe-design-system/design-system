Aspire Advance

Applications open: March 2023

View program details

Advance rewards Year 11 performance with an opportunity to study a first-year uni subject in Year 12. As an alternative to your ATAR, completing the subject unlocks a La Trobe Entrance Score and early conditional offer, while earning you credit towards your degree.

  • Start your application early
  • Receive an early conditional offer
  • Use your La Trobe Entrance Score or your ATAR — whichever is higher
  • First-year subjects are 100% online and free

  • Domestic students completing Year 11 or about to commence Year 12
  • High-achieving students with an average mark of 70% or higher in Year 11

Aspire Achieve Plus

Applications open

View program details

Achieve Plus rewards Year 11 performance with an opportunity to study two first-year uni subjects in Year 12. Completing the subjects boosts your ATAR, unlocks a La Trobe Entrance Score and early conditional offer, and earns you credits towards your degree.

  • Start your application early and receive an early conditional offer
  • Use your La Trobe Entrance Score or your ATAR — whichever is higher
  • First-year subjects are 100% online and free
  • Receive credit for your first-year subjects once you commence your degree

  • Domestic students completing Year 11 or about to commence Year 12
  • High-achieving students with an average mark of 70% or higher in Year 11
  • Students who intend to receive a scored ATAR in Year 12
  • Students with proof of enrolment in at least four VCE unit 3/4 subjects, including English or EAL
